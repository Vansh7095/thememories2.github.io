About us


If you want to build a ship, don't drum up the men to gather wood, divide the work and give orders. Instead teach them to yearn for the vast and endless sea.

SR International School, Bareilly, an English medium CBSE school was established in the year 2008. The school, nurtured under the guardianship of Ms. Ruma Goel, remains firm in its determination to groom students with the finest blend of traditional values, love for cultural heritage and keen desire to keep pace with changing time.

EVER ONWARDS EVER UPWARDS is not just our philosophy but also the driving force behind all our endeavor. The school believes that the pursuit of knowledge is the pursuit of growth in varied aspects.

Children regardless of their age have their own individuality, talents and skills which only a good education system can enhance. To build up children’s comprehensive personality and make them self - reliant individuals of tomorrow, the school offers a conducive learning environment that focusses on bringing out the best in students. The finest team of well - trained and qualified teachers never burdens the students with academics, rather through innovative techniques make classroom learning interesting, giving them ample opportunity for self-expression, innovation, experimentation and understanding of traditional values, needed for holistic development essential to lead a fulfilling life.

AIMS

The school aims

  • To impart education on public school lines, keeping in view the Indian culture and Global perspectives.
  • To equip the youth with qualities of a good citizen besides preparing them for positions of responsibility.
  • To foster desirable attributes of character like honesty, courage, self-confidence, integrity with a view to make them morally strong and socially responsible.
  • To promote creativity and cultivate positive attitude.
  • To transform them into men and women of character who can contribute in bringing needed social change in the country.

OBJECTIVES:

Our objective is to work for a well - rounded holistic education and on making learning a joyful experience. A successful graduate from SR International school will have a different perspective, pragmatic approach and a realistic reasoning to combat life situations and emerge as a winner in all aspects. Our school teaches the students nuances of life and that is what true education is as it stays for a life time.
