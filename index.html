SR International School, which is affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi was established with the aim of imparting quality education. The visionaries and founders Mr. Arvind Kumar Goel and Mrs. Renu Goel are both committed to the cause of providing holistic education. The school will always be indebted to them; it's because of their guiding principles that the school has been able to make a significant mark in and around Bareilly. This commitment to excellence has been taken to further heights by the Managing Director, Mrs. Ruma Goel.The school has been declared as the Best School of Bareilly by the Government of Uttar Pradesh.
